Zhajiangmian (Fried Sauce Noodles)

A classic northern Chinese noodle dish topped with a savory pork and fermented bean sauce.

  1. Boil the noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Stir-fry ground pork with ginger and garlic.
  3. Add fermented soybean paste and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Pour the sauce over the noodles and top with shredded cucumber.

Simple Homemade Pizza

An easy weeknight pizza recipe using store-bought dough.

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the dough and spread tomato sauce evenly.
  3. Add mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden.
