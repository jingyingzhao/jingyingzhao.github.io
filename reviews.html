Restaurant & Dish Reviews

Honest thoughts on restaurants I've visited and dishes I've tried.

Review: Sichuan House

Rating: 4.5 / 5

The mapo tofu here has the perfect balance of numbing spice and savory sauce. Service was quick and the portions are generous. Definitely coming back for their dan dan noodles next time.

Review: Tony's Pizzeria

Rating: 4 / 5

Classic New York style thin crust. The margherita pizza was simple but well executed. A bit pricey for the portion size, but worth it for a special occasion.
